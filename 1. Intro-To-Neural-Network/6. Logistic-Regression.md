## Logistic Regression

- one of the most popular and useful algorithms in Machine Learning.
- and the building block of all that constitutes Deep Learning.
- How it works:  
    - Take your data
    - Pick a random model
    - Calculate the error
    - Minimize the error, and obtain a better model.
